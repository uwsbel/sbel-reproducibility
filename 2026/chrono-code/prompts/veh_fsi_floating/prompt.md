Drive a Polaris wheeled vehicle across a water tank by crossing a floating block that bridges two concrete platforms. The tank is roughly 4m x 2m x 1m (without two lang side walls for see the visual of fsi), filled with SPH fluid (water, density 1000, viscosity 5), with periodic boundaries in the y direction. A low-density (400 kg/m^3) 3.5m x 1.8m x 0.3m rectangular plate floats on the water as a moving bridge between the left and right platforms. There are two 4m x 2m x 0.2m platform at two side of the water tank. The Polaris starts on the left platform braked for the first 0.4s, then throttles up smoothly and drives onto the floating block, which tilts and sinks under the wheels as it crosses. Render with VSG, colour SPH particles by velocity, and camera at the side of the scene. time step is 1e-4 and duration is 3s. The camera should be fixed and be able to look the whole scene here.
